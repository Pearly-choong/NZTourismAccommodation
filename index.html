Kia Ora! Welcome to New Zealand.

This web app helps you to find and book accommdation within New Zealand that fits within your desired needs.


FEATURES:

  1. Choose your destination for booking.
  2. Enter date travel and no of guests.
  3. Filter accomodation types that suit your needs.
  4. Choose meal package options if prefered.
  5. Sort the accomodation by price.
  6. Map view of a list of accomodation available.
  7. Summary of estimated total price.
  8. Reserve your accomodation through link to approprate website.
  • Visit Official NZ Tourism Accommodation Website
  • Download New Zealand Touring Map
